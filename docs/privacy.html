Mind Brother Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 11, 2026
Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Mind Brother ("we," "our," or "us"). Mind Brother is a mental health companion app designed specifically for Black and Brown men. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and respect.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using Mind Brother, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and important service communications
  • Username: Your chosen display name within the app
  • Password: Encrypted and securely stored for account access

2. Profile Information

To personalize your experience, we collect:

  • Name: Your first name or preferred name (optional)
  • Age Range: To provide age-appropriate support and resources
  • Cultural Background: To offer culturally relevant mental health support (optional)
  • Community Identities: Such as veteran status, father, LGBTQ+, etc. (optional)
  • Communication Preferences: How you prefer Amani (our AI companion) to communicate with you

3. Health & Fitness Data

As a mental health app, we collect:

  • Mood Check-Ins: Your daily mood ratings and emotional state
  • Mental Health Tracking: Anxiety levels, stress indicators, and wellness metrics
  • Breathing Exercise Data: Session completion and duration
  • Movement Activity: Workout completion (not biometric data)
  • Crisis Indicators: Automatically detected language patterns that may indicate mental health crisis (for safety purposes only)

4. User-Generated Content

We collect content you create within the app:

  • Chat Messages: Conversations with Amani, our AI mental health companion
  • Journal Entries: Private reflections and notes you write
  • Community Posts: Public posts and comments in discussion forums
  • Feedback: Ratings and comments you provide about the app

5. Device Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device Type: Phone model and operating system version
  • App Version: Which version of Mind Brother you're using
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send you reminders and alerts (if enabled)
  • App Usage Data: Features used, session duration, crash reports

6. Usage Analytics

To improve our app, we collect:

  • Feature Usage: Which parts of the app you use most
  • Session Data: When and how long you use the app
  • Error Reports: Technical issues and crashes
  • User Flow: How you navigate through the app

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  1. Provide Mental Health Support
    • Power conversations with Amani, our AI companion
    • Personalize responses based on your cultural background and preferences
    • Track your mental wellness over time
    • Provide appropriate crisis resources when needed
  2. Improve the App
    • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
    • Fix bugs and technical issues
    • Develop new features based on user needs
  3. Communication
    • Send push notifications for mood check-ins (if enabled)
    • Email important updates about the service
    • Respond to your support requests
  4. Safety & Crisis Prevention
    • Detect potential crisis situations
    • Provide immediate access to crisis resources
    • Connect you with emergency services if necessary

How We Share Your Information

We Share Information With:

1. Service Providers

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Powers Amani's conversations. We share anonymized conversation text only.
  • Supabase: Secure database and authentication. All data is encrypted.
  • ElevenLabs: Text-to-speech for Amani's voice. We share text responses only, no personal data.

2. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights or property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Protect user safety in emergency situations

3. With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly give us permission.

We Do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your personal information
  • ❌ Share your mental health data for advertising
  • ❌ Share your conversations with third parties (except anonymized data to improve AI)
  • ❌ Give employers, insurance companies, or government agencies access to your data

Data Security

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure, industry-standard cloud infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access, strict authentication required
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring and vulnerability testing

Your Responsibility

  • Keep your password secure
  • Don't share your account with others
  • Log out on shared devices

Your Privacy Rights

You Have the Right To:

  1. Access Your Data - Request a copy of all data we have about you
  2. Correct Your Data - Update inaccurate information in Settings
  3. Delete Your Data - Delete individual messages, journal entries, or your entire account
  4. Export Your Data - Download your chat history and journal entries
  5. Opt-Out - Disable notifications, analytics, or specific features

How to Exercise Your Rights

In-App: Settings → Account Settings → Privacy
Email: privacy@mindbrother.app
Response Time: We'll respond within 30 days

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Obligations: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Anonymized Data: We may retain anonymized analytics data indefinitely

Children's Privacy

Mind Brother is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and data immediately.

If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at privacy@mindbrother.app.

International Users

Mind Brother is based in the United States. If you're using our app from outside the US:

  • Your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR for EU users
  • We use standard contractual clauses for international data transfers

For EU Users (GDPR Rights)

Under GDPR, you have additional rights:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

Contact us at gdpr@mindbrother.app for GDPR-related requests.

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. If you disagree with changes, you must stop using the app and may request deletion of your data.
