<h2 id="introduction">Introduction</h2>

<p>Welcome to Mind Brother (&quot;we,&quot; &quot;our,&quot; or &quot;us&quot;). Mind Brother is a mental health companion app designed specifically for men. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and respect.</p>

<p>This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the &quot;App&quot;). By using Mind Brother, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.</p>

<hr>

<h2 id="information-we-collect">Information We Collect</h2>

<h3 id="1-account-information">1. Account Information</h3>

<p>When you create an account, we collect:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>Email Address</strong>: Used for account creation, authentication, and important service communications</li>

<li><strong>Username</strong>: Your chosen display name within the app</li>

<li><strong>Password</strong>: Encrypted and securely stored for account access</li>

</ul>

<h3 id="2-profile-information">2. Profile Information</h3>

<p>To personalize your experience, we collect:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>Name</strong>: Your first name or preferred name (optional)</li>

<li><strong>Age Range</strong>: To provide age-appropriate support and resources</li>

<li><strong>Cultural Background</strong>: To offer culturally relevant mental health support (optional)</li>

<li><strong>Community Identities</strong>: Such as veteran status, father, LGBTQ+, etc. (optional)</li>

<li><strong>Communication Preferences</strong>: How you prefer Amani (our AI companion) to communicate with you</li>

</ul>

<h3 id="3-health-fitness-data">3. Health &amp; Fitness Data</h3>

<p>As a mental health app, we collect:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>Mood Check-Ins</strong>: Your daily mood ratings and emotional state</li>

<li><strong>Mental Health Tracking</strong>: Anxiety levels, stress indicators, and wellness metrics</li>

<li><strong>Breathing Exercise Data</strong>: Session completion and duration</li>

<li><strong>Movement Activity</strong>: Workout completion (not biometric data)</li>

<li><strong>Crisis Indicators</strong>: Automatically detected language patterns that may indicate mental health crisis (for safety purposes only)</li>

</ul>

<h3 id="4-user-generated-content">4. User-Generated Content</h3>

<p>We collect content you create within the app:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>Chat Messages</strong>: Conversations with Amani, our AI mental health companion</li>

<li><strong>Journal Entries</strong>: Private reflections and notes you write</li>

<li><strong>Community Posts</strong>: Public posts and comments in discussion forums</li>

<li><strong>Feedback</strong>: Ratings and comments you provide about the app</li>

</ul>

<h3 id="5-device-information">5. Device Information</h3>

<p>We automatically collect:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>Device Type</strong>: iPhone model and iOS version</li>

<li><strong>App Version</strong>: Which version of Mind Brother you&#39;re using</li>

<li><strong>Push Notification Tokens</strong>: To send you reminders and alerts (if enabled)</li>

<li><strong>App Usage Data</strong>: Features used, session duration, crash reports</li>

</ul>

<h3 id="6-usage-analytics">6. Usage Analytics</h3>

<p>To improve our app, we collect:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>Feature Usage</strong>: Which parts of the app you use most</li>

<li><strong>Session Data</strong>: When and how long you use the app</li>

<li><strong>Error Reports</strong>: Technical issues and crashes</li>

<li><strong>User Flow</strong>: How you navigate through the app</li>

</ul>

<h2 id="data-security">Data Security</h2>

<h3 id="how-we-protect-your-information">How We Protect Your Information</h3>

<ul>

<li><strong>Encryption</strong>: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest</li>

<li><strong>Secure Authentication</strong>: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text</li>

<li><strong>Access Controls</strong>: Only authorized personnel can access user data, and only when necessary</li>

<li><strong>Regular Security Audits</strong>: We regularly review and update our security practices</li>

<li><strong>Row-Level Security</strong>: Database policies ensure users can only access their own data</li>

</ul>

<h3 id="ai-data-processing">AI Data Processing</h3>

<ul>

<li>Conversations with Amani are processed through Anthropic&#39;s Claude API</li>

<li>We do not store your API conversations longer than necessary</li>

<li>Anthropic does not train their models on your data (per their Enterprise agreement)</li>

<li>See Anthropic&#39;s privacy policy: <a href="https://www.anthropic.com/privacy">https://www.anthropic.com/privacy</a></li>

</ul>

<h2 id="crisis-situations">Crisis Situations</h2>

<h3 id="when-we-may-contact-you">When We May Contact You</h3>

<p>If our AI detects language indicating:</p>

<ul>

<li>Imminent risk of self-harm or suicide</li>

<li>Plans to harm others</li>

<li>Severe mental health crisis</li>

</ul>

<p>We will:</p>

<ol>

<li>Immediately provide crisis resources (988 Lifeline, Crisis Text Line)</li>

<li>May attempt to connect you with a professional counselor (if available)</li>

<li>In extreme cases, may contact emergency services if we believe there&#39;s imminent danger</li>

</ol>

<p><strong>Mind Brother is NOT a crisis service. If you&#39;re in crisis, call 988 or text HOME to 741741.</strong></p>
